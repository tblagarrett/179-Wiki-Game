The House Killer

 Robert “Bobby” Johnson was born in Kidneymore, California, on October 25th, 1955. He was an American serial killer, and was found to murder four individuals between the dates of October 28th, 1983 to October 31st, 1983. Bobby was nicknamed the “House Killer” as all of his victims were previous housemates/classmates from his time at the local community college. A majority of his killings including the dismemberment and displaying of his former housemate alongside a note found along the body. All bodies were found within the Santa Cruz area.

 Bobby was never persecuted, as on October 31st he was found attempting to display a victim alongside the highway 17 when he was noticed by police. He attempted to flee the scene, leading to a police chase along the highway. Bobby drove the car off the side of the road, resulting in a crash which took his life on impact. Due to his sudden death, the motiviations behind his “Saw”-esque killings is still unknown.
