Camp Daybreak

 Camp Daybreak is a camp that lies within the woods just outside of Santa Cruz. The collection of small cabins is a popular spot for local children to participate in summer camp. There are a number of activities that campers can enjoy. Several auxiliary closets reside around the camp to hold all of the equipment. Lots of activities are centered around the nearby Loch Lomond.
